# Consultation System

Date: 2026-07-11

## Founder flow

Consultation moves from Orient -> Gather -> Map -> Move -> Return. It can involve the owner, a Group NUR room, a collaborator, or a vetted expert, while preserving source boundaries and returning a real Outcome to the originating Orbit.

## Track A truth

The five-stage V197 path remains visually present, but `v197Hydration.ts` disables stage/action controls and replaces fake room copy. No active participants, experts, replies, scheduling, or payments are claimed.

## Track B backend

- consultation rooms and stage transitions;
- invitations, memberships, roles, consent, and expiry;
- bounded source bundle derived from an Orbit/Capsule-style grant;
- questions, responses, disagreement, witness-only contributions, and provenance;
- scheduling and optional payment records isolated from private context;
- returned decisions, references, constraints, Plan moves, and Outcomes;
- moderation, audit, revocation, export, and retention controls.

## AI role

NUR may frame the question, separate observed/inferred/hypothesis, translate approved contributions, identify disagreement, and propose a next move. It must cite only room-approved sources and cannot silently write back to Personal Orbit; the owner approves captured material.

## Acceptance

Membership/role RLS, revoked-room denial, bounded retrieval, translation provenance, stage transition validation, no witness-content promotion without consent, Outcome write-back approval, notification caps, payment failure safety, and full owner/member E2E are required before enabling canonical V197 controls.
